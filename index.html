LLC "M-LIT" (NZTA) (formerly Nikopol Foundry and Mechanical Plant "Bolshevik") is a large specialized enterprise known in Ukraine and abroad for the production of shut-off valves. The company was created more than 115 years ago. During this period, the plant went from producing furnace castings to producing cast iron valves used as shut-off devices on pipelines to shut off the working environment. The company produces cast iron valves with a nominal bore from 50 mm to 500 mm, grades 30ch6br and 30ch906br for water and steam, with manual control and electric drive; 30ch6bk for petroleum products and oils; 30ch47bk4 and 30ch47br for natural gas and water with nominal bore from 50 mm to 150 mm. "M-LIT" today is technical equipment of production with universal and specialized equipment, qualified specialists, which, combined with extensive experience of the plant in this industry allows the enterprise team to produce a wide range of products pipeline fittings. Contact us - and we will fulfill your order quickly and qualitatively. Products bearing the logo of our company can be found everywhere - from huge metallurgical plants, thermal power plants, quarries to irrigation systems in rural economy, public utilities of any settlement in the CIS countries. And products are everywhere Our enterprise operates efficiently and reliably.
